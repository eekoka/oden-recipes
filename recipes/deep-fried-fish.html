Deep Fried Fish Recipe

Fish burgers or any other processed fish are out of scope here. I am referring to real whole fish, preferably directly from the ocean or river. When it comes to recipes, fried fish is as easy as it gets.

Fried fish can be served with fried plantains, and hot pepper sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fish (as many as you want to eat): Tilapia and trout are some of the best fishes to fry.
  • 2 L vegetable oil or other oil for deep frying
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Clean the fish:
    • Remove the scales
    • Remove the gut
    • Remove the fins
    • Rense and drain
  2. If the fish is too big, cut it into two or three pieces.
  3. Sprinkle salt generously all over the fish.
  4. Sprinkle salt generously all over the fish.
  5. Heat the oil up (medium-high heat) in a deep pot till the oil is hot enough for frying.
  6. Deep-Fry the fish until golden brown.
