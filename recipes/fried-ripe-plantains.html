Fried Ripe Plantains Recipe

Fried ripe plantain is a sweet snack. As the name suggests, it is simply deep-fried ripe plantains. And no! It is not a banana. Google the differences between plantain and banana.

Like puff-puff, fried ripe plantains can be served with hot pepper sauce and/or beans.

Ingredients

This is a simple dish. Only a couple of ingredients are needed:

  • Ripe plantains (as much as you want)
  • 2 L vegetable oil or other oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into slices - see the example in the above image.
  2. Heat the oil up (medium-high heat) in a deep fryer (or deep pot) till the oil is hot enough for frying.
  3. Fry the sliced plantains in small batches, according to the size of your deep fryer or pot. Don't fry too many at a time.
  4. Fry the plantains until light golden brown.
